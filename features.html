Features

  • User-Friendly Interface

    1. Click to add and drag dots
    2. Select multiple dots at once
    3. Toggle dot names and grid lines

  • High functionality

    1. Detailed dot information
    2. Dot name toggles(C1, A4, etc.)
    3. Automatic notifications for possible collisions during moves

  • Widely Accessible

    1. Support for both High School and College hashes
    2. Print dot sheets for everyone with a single click
    3. Movable panel containing useful page information
